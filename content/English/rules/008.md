Meo:
  - Mark advertisements with a label that identifies them as such (advertisement, ad, partnership, etc.).
  - Where possible, graphically separate advertising from the rest of the content.
Controle:
  - Identify advertising content.
  - Ensure that all advertising spaces are clearly distinguishable from the rest of the content and include a label that allows them to be identified without ambiguity. Typically, the word “advertisement” displayed above or below the content concerned.
