  - Label advertisements with a term that clearly identifies them (e.g., "Advertisement", "Ad", "Sponsored content").
  - Visually separate advertising from the rest of the content whenever possible.

  - Locate any advertising content.
  - Verify that all advertising areas are visually distinct from the rest of the content and include a label that identifies them unambiguously, such as the word "Advertisement" displayed above or below the relevant content.
