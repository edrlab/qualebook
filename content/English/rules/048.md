objectif:
  - Inform users of what to expect before starting a download, preventing surprises like longer-than-expected wait times.
  - Allow users to manage their resources, such as bandwidth and storage space, by knowing file sizes in advance.
Meo:
  - List all available downloadable files, determine their size in kilobytes (KB) or megabytes (MB), and include this information clearly within the link text or description.
  - Document this practice in internal guidelines for content teams.
Controle:
  - Verify that the file size is specified for every download link.
  - Perform regular audits to ensure that new or updated downloads comply with this requirement.
