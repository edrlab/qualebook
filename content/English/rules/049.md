objectif:
  - Allow users to know what to expect before downloading a document, avoiding confusion or frustration.
  - Help readers quickly identify files available in their preferred language, improving the reading experience.
  - Reduce the risk of errors and unnecessary downloads, improving efficiency and user satisfaction.
Meo:
  - Explicitly state the language of every downloadable file.
  - Use the lang attribute to specify the language of HTML elements, including links to downloadable files.
  - Clearly indicate the file's language within the link text or an associated description.

  - Ensure that every download link includes a clear indication of the file's language within the descriptive text.
  - Use language detection tools to partially automate this task.
