  - "Do not include elements on the page that trigger the playback of uncontrollable video, for example with the HTML video element with the `autoplay` attribute, or without the `controls` attribute."
Controle:
  - On each page containing video content, check that there is no video content that is automatically activated when the page loads.
  - Verify that there is no video content activated unpredictably as a result of an action taken by the person.
