objectif:
  - Allow users to control the volume during playback.
  - Prevent unexpected audio playback that could disrupt or startle the user.
  - Improve accessibility for users with disabilities, particularly those using screen readers.
Meo:
  - Ensure the HTML <audio> element includes the "controls" attribute and excludes the "autoplay" attribute.
  - Add the <meta property="schema:accessibilityHazard">noSoundHazard</meta> metadata to the OPF file.
Controle:
  - Verify that no audio content starts playing automatically when the page or document is opened.
